# SkillUp 2027: The Forest Ascension (Conqueror Edition) 🌳🔥⚔️

A high-performance, gamified life-management dashboard designed to accelerate the journey toward becoming a Computer Scientist by 2027. This ecosystem balances disciplined academic preparation with career-focused mission tracking.

---

## 🛡️ Project Rebirth: The Conqueror's Start

After Day Zero (April 10th), the dashboard was transformed into a **Relentless Growth System**.

### 1. The Phoenix Reset Protocol
- **Trigger:** Interactive "Rebirth" button in the HUD.
- **Goal:** Clear distractions, reset streaks, and focus on the April 10th restart.
- **Rank:** Unlocks the glowing **"Phoenix Reborn"** status.

### 2. The Tree of Resilience
A living visual of your discipline on the world map.
- **Growth Logic:** Sprouts leaves for every day of your active streak.
- **Persistence:** Missing 2 days resets the tree to a seed.
- **Phases:** 🌱 Seed -> 🌿 Sapling -> 🌳 Mature.

## ⚙️ The Engine

- **5 AM Purpose Routine:** Calibrated for a **4:45 AM Rise** and **5:00 AM Strike** (Deep Work start).
